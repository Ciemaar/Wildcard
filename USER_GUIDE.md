# User Guide

Welcome to the **Wildcard Management System (WMS)**!
This application helps manage prompts for "The Wildcard Project" and effortlessly generates print-ready cards.

## 1. Idea Dashboard
The Idea Dashboard is your central hub for managing your prompt catalog.

### Viewing Prompts
- On the homepage, you will see a table of all available prompts.
- Use the **Filter** dropdowns at the top to narrow down the list by specific **Status** (e.g., `DRAFT`, `APPROVED`) or **Category**.

### Adding a New Prompt
1. Click the blue **Add Prompt** button.
2. Fill out the "Prompt Text", "Category", and "Difficulty" (1-3 scale).
3. Click **Add**. The new prompt will immediately appear in your table as a `DRAFT`.

### Updating Prompt Status
To approve a prompt for printing, simply click the dropdown menu in the "Status" column of the respective row and select `APPROVED`. The system updates immediately.

### Deleting Prompts
If a prompt is no longer needed, click the red **Delete** button at the end of its row to permanently remove it.

---

## 2. Print Studio
The Print Studio allows you to assemble `APPROVED` prompts into downloadable PDFs formatted with physical cut-lines.

### Creating a Batch
1. Navigate to the **Print Studio** via the top navigation bar.
2. You will see a list of only your `APPROVED` prompts.
3. Enter a **Batch Name** (e.g., "Launch Deck v1").
4. Select the prompts you wish to print by checking the boxes next to them. *Tip: The standard layout prints 9 cards per US Letter page. The system will warn you if your selection is not a multiple of 9 to prevent empty cards from rendering.*
5. Click **Generate Batch**.

### Downloading the PDF
Upon successfully creating a batch, a success message will appear containing a **Download PDF** link.
Clicking this link will instantly download a perfectly formatted PDF document with 3x3 grids of your selected prompts, including crop marks, sequential IDs, and custom branding—ready for your printer!
